The bird game

What you will need

Some tape, yarn, scissors, and a lot of trash to build from.

  • How to play

    For this fantastic bird game, you will need at least 2 players, you can play with as many as you want! so get all your friends and classmates together the more the marrier

  • Building Materials

    First you take everything you have gathered from home, use scissors, tape and whatever you need to make a birdfeeder. You want something strong that can hold up the feeder, so bring some sturdy cardboard, a piece of wood, or some metal. You decide if you want to just have a board, a small house, or anything in between, so use your imagination

  • How to score points

    The more attractive your birdfeeder are to birds, the more points you are going to score. You score points by the amount of birds that land on your board. If the bird flies off, but comes back and land on your board again you score another point.

  • How to win

    The person that has the most points after 10 minutes wins, timer starts when the first bird lands on any board.
