to a website dedicated to UN developement goals, There are a total of 17 goals For this project I chose goal number 11,

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable. To help reach this goal I developed a game for everyone,

The goal

To accomplish this goal I made a game constructed to use waste material from home, to create something useful, a birdfeeder!

Not only do you get to recycle your garbage in to something useful, you also get to participate in a fun game with your friends or classmates! To read more about how to play the game, visit The game
